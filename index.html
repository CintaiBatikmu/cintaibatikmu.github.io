CintaiBatikmu / Is

CintaiBatikmu is from three words "Cintai" means Love, "Batik" is a cloth that is traditionally made using a manual waxresist dyeing technique. Batik Original from Java (Indonesia). Indonesia is not just Java, Indonesia consists of many islands, hundreds of distinct native ethnic, traditional cloth, linguistic groups, and Batik is one of them. And the last word is "mu" means "You", so CintaiBatikMu is "Love your Batik". You look more beautiful when you wear Batik :)
